# Code of Conduct

Gantral follows the CNCF Code of Conduct, which is based on the Contributor Covenant.

We are committed to providing a welcoming, professional, and respectful environment for all contributors.

---

## Our Standards

We expect all participants to:

- Be respectful and considerate
- Assume good intent
- Engage constructively, even when disagreeing
- Focus on technical and architectural merit
- Respect project scope and governance decisions

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or sustained disruption of project discussions.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

They may take appropriate action, including moderation, warnings, or removal from the project, to ensure a healthy community.

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to:

**conduct@gantral.org** *(or a monitored project email)*

Reports will be handled confidentially and reviewed by the maintainers.

---
