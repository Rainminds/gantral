Welcome to Gantral

Gantral is the open-source AI Execution Control Plane. It allows teams and enterprises to standardize, govern, and audit AI agent workflows across teams.

"We don’t help you build agents. We help you run AI safely across your organization."

Executive Context​

For readers evaluating Gantral from an enterprise, platform, risk, or regulatory perspective, we maintain a small set of executive briefings that provide higher-level context on:

  • what the system is and is not,
  • how workflows change after adoption,
  • how it scales across teams,
  • and how execution-time governance is enforced.

These materials are not required to understand, use, or contribute to the open-source project.
